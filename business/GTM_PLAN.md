## Revenue Model Breakdown

### Consumer SaaS
- **Basic:** $19/mo - Single alert, email notifications
- **Premium:** $49/mo - Unlimited alerts, SMS, priority support
- **Target mix:** 80% Basic, 20% Premium

### Enterprise/White-Label
- **Broker Package:** $500/mo - White-label calculator + alerts
- **Lender Package:** $2,000/mo - API access + lead attribution
- **Target:** 20 enterprise customers by EOY1

### Lead Generation
- **Qualified Lead:** $25-50 per lead to partner lenders
- **Conversion tracking:** Attribution via unique codes
- **Target:** 500 leads/mo by Q4

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Nail product-market fit fast (Month 1-3)**
   - Talk to 50+ potential customers
   - Iterate on pricing and features
   - Find repeatable acquisition channel

2. **Build content moat early (SEO takes 6-12 months)**
   - Publish 2-3 quality posts per week
   - Target long-tail mortgage keywords
   - Build backlinks through partnerships

3. **Validate unit economics before scaling ads**
   - LTV:CAC must be >3x
   - Payback period <6 months
   - Don't scale unprofitable channels

4. **Move fast on international (first-mover advantage)**
   - UK market similar dynamics
   - Canada/Australia English-speaking
   - Localize rates and regulations

5. **Build lender partnerships (unlock lead gen revenue)**
   - High-margin revenue stream
   - Aligns incentives with users
   - Creates competitive moat

6. **Don't raise too much too early (dilution)**
   - Bootstrap as long as possible
   - Revenue is the best fundraising leverage
   - Keep optionality for profitable exit

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Rate API reliability | Multiple data sources, caching, fallbacks |
| Customer churn | Onboarding flow, engagement emails, value reminders |
| Competition | Speed to market, SEO moat, lender relationships |
| Regulatory changes | Legal review, compliance monitoring |
| Economic downturn | Diversify to lender partnerships (counter-cyclical) |
