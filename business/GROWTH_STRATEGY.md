# Customer Acquisition and Growth Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a multi-channel customer acquisition strategy designed to achieve sustainable growth from launch to scale. The strategy prioritizes high-ROI organic channels while building toward paid acquisition once unit economics are validated.

**Target Metrics:**
- Month 12: 50K monthly visitors
- CAC target: $10-50 depending on channel
- LTV target: >$300 for paid acquisition viability
- Viral coefficient target: 1.2-1.5

---

## Growth Channels Analysis

### Channel 1: Content Marketing (Organic)

**Strategy:** SEO-optimized blog posts targeting refinance-related keywords

**Execution:**
- Target keywords: "should I refinance", "refinance calculator", "refinance break-even", "mortgage rate comparison"
- Content types: How-to guides, calculators, market analysis, case studies
- Publishing cadence: 2-4 posts per week initially

**Metrics:**
- Expected: 50K visitors/month by month 12
- Conversion: 2-5% visitors to free users
- Free to paid conversion: 10%
- CAC: $10-20 per user

**Timeline:** 6-12 months to scale

**Priority:** HIGH (building now)

---

### Channel 2: Paid Search (SEM)

**Strategy:** Google Ads targeting high-intent refinance keywords

**Target Keywords:**
- "refinance calculator"
- "should I refinance"
- "mortgage rates today"
- "refinance break even calculator"
- "home refinance options"

**Execution:**
- Start with small budget ($500-1000/month) to validate
- A/B test ad copy and landing pages
- Optimize for conversion, not just clicks
- Retarget website visitors

**Metrics:**
- CAC: $50-150 per user
- ROI positive only if LTV > $300

**Timeline:** Immediate (once product-market fit confirmed)

**Priority:** MEDIUM (start after product-market fit)

---

### Channel 3: Affiliate Partnerships

**Strategy:** Partner with personal finance content creators

**Target Partners:**
- Personal finance blogs (NerdWallet-style sites)
- YouTube finance channels
- Podcast hosts (personal finance, real estate)
- Financial influencers

**Compensation Model:**
- Revenue share: 20-30% of subscription
- OR: Lead generation fee ($5-20 per qualified lead)
- OR: Flat fee per signup with minimum commitment

**Metrics:**
- CAC: $0 upfront (performance-based)
- Expected partner count: 10-20 by month 6

**Timeline:** 3-6 months to build network

**Priority:** HIGH (scalable, low-risk)

---

### Channel 4: Referral Program

**Strategy:** Incentivize existing users to refer friends and family

**Options:**
1. **Cash Incentive:** Give $10, Get $10
2. **Free Trial Extension:** 1 month free for referrer and referee
3. **Feature Unlock:** Premium features for successful referrals

**Implementation:**
- Unique referral links per user
- Social sharing buttons (email, SMS, social)
- Referral tracking dashboard
- Automated reward fulfillment

**Metrics:**
- CAC: $10-20 per referred user
- Viral coefficient target: 1.2-1.5 (each user brings 1.2-1.5 new users)

**Timeline:** Launch with product

**Priority:** HIGH (build into product from day 1)

---

### Channel 5: Social Media

**Strategy:** Build community presence on finance-focused platforms

**Platforms:**

**Reddit**
- r/personalfinance (16M members)
- r/mortgages
- r/FirstTimeHomeBuyer
- r/RealEstate
- Strategy: Provide value first, promote subtly

**Twitter/X**
- Finance community (#FinTwit)
- Real estate professionals
- Strategy: Thought leadership, market commentary

**TikTok**
- Financial education content
- Quick tips on refinancing
- Strategy: Entertaining, educational short-form content

**Metrics:**
- CAC: $20-50 (organic content time investment)
- Focus on engagement over followers

**Timeline:** Ongoing

**Priority:** MEDIUM (supplement to primary channels)

---

### Channel 6: Strategic Partnerships

**Strategy:** B2B2C partnerships for distribution leverage

**Target Partners:**

**Real Estate Agents**
- Referral arrangement for homebuyers
- White-label calculator for agent websites
- Co-marketing opportunities

**Financial Advisors**
- White-label solution for client portfolios
- Referral fees for qualified leads
- Educational content partnerships

**Mortgage Brokers**
- Lead sharing arrangements
- Calculator integration for their sites
- Revenue share on converted leads

**Banks/Credit Unions**
- Co-marketing campaigns
- Calculator widget for their customers
- Potential acquisition channel

**Metrics:**
- Varies by partnership structure
- Focus on lifetime partnerships over one-time deals

**Timeline:** 3-6 months to establish first partnerships

**Priority:** HIGH (B2B2C leverage for scale)

---

### Channel 7: Email Marketing

**Strategy:** Build email list via free calculator, nurture to paid conversion

**List Building:**
- Gate premium features behind email signup
- Offer personalized rate alerts
- Free educational content downloads
- Newsletter subscription

**Nurture Sequence (10 emails):**
1. Welcome + Quick Win (rate check)
2. Educational: How refinancing works
3. Case Study: Real savings example
4. Calculator deep dive
5. Market update + rates
6. Common refinancing mistakes
7. Premium feature teaser
8. Limited time offer
9. Social proof + testimonials
10. Final conversion push

**Metrics:**
- Email open rate target: 25-30%
- Click rate target: 3-5%
- Conversion rate: 5-10% to paid
- CAC: $5-10

**Timeline:** Build immediately, iterate continuously

**Priority:** HIGH (owned channel, high ROI)

## Growth Playbook

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)

**Focus:** Product-market fit and infrastructure

**Actions:**
- Perfect core calculator experience
- Build email capture and nurture sequence
- Launch referral program (v1)
- Start content engine (first 10-20 blog posts)
- Set up analytics and attribution tracking
- Establish baseline metrics

**Goals:**
- 1,000 email subscribers
- Validate core value proposition
- Identify highest-converting user segments

---

### Phase 2: Organic Growth (Months 4-6)

**Focus:** Scale organic channels

**Actions:**
- SEO content scaling (50+ posts)
- Affiliate partnerships (10-20 partners)
- Reddit/social community building
- Email list growth to 10K+
- Iterate on referral program
- Begin strategic partnership outreach

**Goals:**
- 10K monthly visitors
- 500 paid users
- Validate CAC across channels

---

### Phase 3: Paid Acquisition (Months 7-12)

**Focus:** Layer in paid channels once unit economics proven

**Actions:**
- Google Ads launch (validated keywords)
- Influencer partnerships (paid)
- Strategic B2B partnerships signed
- Expand affiliate network
- Scale winning organic channels

**Goals:**
- 50K monthly visitors
- 2,000 paid users
- CAC < LTV/3 across all channels

---

### Phase 4: Scale (Year 2-3)

**Focus:** Market leadership and expansion

**Actions:**
- TV/podcast advertising (if unit economics support)
- International expansion (Canada, UK, Australia)
- White-label SaaS product for partners
- Enterprise/B2B product line
- Potential acquisition of smaller competitors

**Goals:**
- 500K monthly visitors
- 50,000+ paid users
- Market leader in refinance calculator space

## Risk Mitigation

### Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| High CAC on paid channels | Burns cash without ROI | Start small, validate before scaling |
| Low conversion rates | Affects all channel ROI | Continuous product improvement, A/B testing |
| Affiliate fraud | Wasted referral payouts | Fraud detection, quality checks |
| Platform dependency | Algorithm changes tank traffic | Diversify channels, build owned (email) |
| Competition | CAC increases, margins compress | Focus on differentiation, not price |
