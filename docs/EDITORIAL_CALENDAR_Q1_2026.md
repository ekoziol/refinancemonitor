### Keyword Research Framework

Each post targets:
- **1 Primary Keyword**: High-intent, 1,000-10,000 monthly searches
- **3-5 Secondary Keywords**: Long-tail variations
- **1-2 Question Keywords**: Featured snippet opportunities

### Internal Linking Strategy

| Post Type | Links To |
|-----------|----------|
| Rate Analysis | Calculator tools, How-To guides |
| Regional Guides | Related regional posts, Rate Analysis |

| Industry News | Rate Analysis, relevant How-To |
| Case Studies | Related How-To guides, Calculator tools |

### Featured Snippet Opportunities

Target "How to" and "What is" queries with:
- Numbered lists (steps)
- Definition paragraphs (40-60 words)
- Comparison tables
- Quick answer boxes
