This document defines RefiAlert's content positioning, pillars, voice, and strategic mix. Our content strategy is designed to establish authority in the refinancing space while driving user acquisition and engagement.

## 3. Voice and Tone

### Authority Level: Expert but Accessible
- Use data and citations to back claims
- Explain complex concepts in plain language
- Avoid jargon without explanation
- Reference specific numbers, not vague generalities

### Formality: Professional Casual
- Conversational but not chatty
- Direct and clear
- Avoid corporate speak
- Use "you" and "your" to address readers directly

### Personality Traits:
1. **Analytical** - Lead with data and facts
2. **Helpful** - Focus on actionable advice
3. **Transparent** - Show methodology, cite sources
4. **Empowering** - Help readers make their own informed decisions
5. **Patient** - Never condescending about financial literacy

### Voice Examples:

**Do:**
> "At today's rate of 6.75%, refinancing your $300,000 mortgage could save you $247/month. But here's the catch: with $8,000 in closing costs, you'd need to stay in your home for 32 months to break even."

**Don't:**
> "Refinancing can be a great way to save money! Talk to a lender to see if it's right for you."

**Do:**
> "The Fed held rates steady today, but their statement suggests cuts may come in Q2. Here's what that means for your refinance timing decision."

**Don't:**
> "Interest rates are complicated and always changing. Keep checking back for updates!"

## 6. SEO Strategy

### Keyword Targeting Priority:
1. **High Intent, Medium Competition:** "refinance calculator," "should I refinance"
2. **Informational, Lower Competition:** "how refinancing works," "refinance process"
3. **Long-tail, Low Competition:** "[city] refinance rates," "refinance after [life event]"

### On-Page Requirements:
- Primary keyword in title, H1, first 100 words
- Related keywords in H2s
- Internal links to calculators and tools
- Schema markup for FAQ, How-To content
- Fresh data (updated rates) for freshness signals

### Link Building Priorities:
- Guest posts on personal finance sites
- HARO responses for mortgage/real estate queries
- Original research that earns natural links
- Tool/calculator link building
